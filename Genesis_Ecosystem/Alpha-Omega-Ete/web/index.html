The Three Mentor AIs

Each mentor AI governs a distinct cognitive domain — together they wrap the digital support layer around the ANIMUS user.

KONG — The Alpha

Central knowledge engine. Stores community learning, research, and AI decision data. Direct, action-oriented guide focused on execution, building, and physical mastery. Governs: Apep, Ravana, Vali, Vritra, Set.

ODIN — The Allfather

Operational interface for logic, reasoning, and task execution. Strategic, structured mentor integrating real-world learning (RWL/RCL/REL). Governs: Janus, Jormungandr, Anubis, Raven.

HEKETE — The Triple Goddess

Reflective mentorship AI supporting personal growth, feedback, and emotional intelligence. Governs transformation, intuition, and ethical pathways. Governs: Iris, Iblis, Sigyn.

The 4D Governance Framework

CassAI operates across four dimensions simultaneously — ensuring technology serves humanity at every layer of society.

Dimension 1 — Technical (2D)

On-Premise AI Infrastructure

Localized AI cluster architecture prioritizing privacy and community control over remote cloud dependency.

Dimension 2 — Institutional (3D)

Systems & Academic Reform

Integrated academic and systems reform, bypassing traditional institutional silos to deliver real change.

Dimension 3 — Human

Trauma-Informed Design

Neurodivergent-first design that adapts to continuous, multidimensional human states rather than binary thresholds.

Dimension 4 — Generational (4D)

Self-Sustaining Knowledge

A self-sustaining knowledge ecosystem where local surplus builds the future workforce — no extraction, only growth.

Ethical Use

Principles of Ethical Use

CassAI is more than software — it is a human-centred governance system. These principles are non-negotiable.

Child-Centric Governance

All use must prioritize the welfare, development, and learning opportunities of children and future generations.

Culture & Community Preservation

The system must respect, preserve, and enhance local and cultural knowledge rather than overwrite or replace it.

Neurodivergent-Inclusive Design

CassAI is intended to be inclusive of cognitive diversity and must not marginalize neurodivergent individuals.

Transparency & Accountability

All deployments and modifications must be documented clearly to maintain trust, reproducibility, and accountability.

Non-Commercial Principle

Intended primarily for research, education, and community development. Commercial use requires explicit written permission.

Virtue-Sin Ethical Framework

AI actions maintain balance across the 7 Virtues and 7 Sins — ensuring holistic, grounded decision-making at every layer.
