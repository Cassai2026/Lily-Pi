## Purpose

CassAI is more than software — it is a **human-centred AI governance system** designed to:

- Protect and empower **children and future generations**  
- Preserve **culture and community knowledge**  
- Support **ethical AI development and neurodivergent-inclusive learning**  

These guidelines define **the ethical use of CassAI**, ensuring it is applied in alignment with its intended purpose.

---

## Principles of Ethical Use

1. **Child-Centric Governance**  
   All use must prioritize the welfare, development, and learning opportunities of children and future generations.

2. **Culture & Community Preservation**  
   The system must respect, preserve, and enhance local and cultural knowledge rather than overwrite or replace it.

3. **Neurodivergent-Inclusive Design**  
   CassAI is intended to be inclusive of cognitive diversity and must not marginalize or discriminate against neurodivergent individuals.

4. **Transparency & Accountability**  
   All deployments and modifications should be documented clearly to maintain trust, reproducibility, and accountability.

5. **Non-Commercial Principle**  
   The system is intended primarily for research, education, and community development. Commercial exploitation requires explicit written permission from the copyright holder.

6. **Virtue-Sin Ethical Framework**  
   CassAI’s AI actions must maintain balance across the 7 Virtues (prudence, justice, temperance, courage, faith, hope, charity/love) and 7 Sins (pride, envy, wrath, sloth, greed, gluttony, lust).

7. **Generational Tetrahedron Principle**  
   Every action, system decision, or deployment must support the stability and flourishing of the next generation, symbolized in CassAI’s tetrahedron model.

## Recommended Usage

- **Education:** Incorporate into classrooms, workshops, and community learning programs.  
- **Research:** Study ethical AI, neurodivergent-inclusive systems, and human-centred governance.  
- **Community Hubs:** Deploy CassAI in local tech and learning hubs to empower children and adults alike.  

---

## Prohibited Usage

- Any deployment that **exploits or harms children or communities**.  
- Any use that **disrespects cultural knowledge or marginalizes neurodivergent individuals**.  
- Unauthorized commercial sale, licensing, or monetization of CassAI systems or derivative works.  
- Modifications or integrations that **subvert or bypass ethical constraints** built into CassAI’s AI layers.

---

## Enforcement

Violations of these ethical guidelines may result in:

- Revocation of access to community or collaborative resources  
- Public notice of misuse  
- Legal action in cases of commercial exploitation without permission
