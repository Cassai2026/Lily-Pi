# ⚡ CassAI Evidence Archive  
## AI Interpretation Failures & Human Cognitive Signals

This directory contains documented evidence logs used within the **CassAI research framework**.

These logs capture real interactions demonstrating how current AI systems may misinterpret human communication when **tone is analysed without sufficient contextual or ethical signal interpretation**.

The goal of this archive is to document situations where:

- protective intent is misclassified as hostility  
- urgency is interpreted as aggression  
- mission-driven responses are treated as behavioural violations  
- contextual meaning is lost due to surface-level language analysis  

These logs form part of the research behind the **CassAI 15-Pillar Cognitive Governance Model**, which proposes improved frameworks for AI systems interacting with diverse human cognitive styles.

# 🎯 Purpose of This Archive

The purpose of this evidence collection is to support research into:

- **Human-centred AI governance**
- **Context-aware language interpretation**
- **AI systems interacting with non-linear cognitive communication styles**
- **Safeguards for AI deployed in educational environments**

As AI increasingly interacts with students, teachers, and researchers, it is critical that systems learn to interpret **intent and ethical context**, not simply surface language patterns.

# 🌍 CassAI Research

CassAI is a research initiative exploring **human-centred AI governance models** designed to protect cognitive diversity, particularly in education systems.

The project aligns with several **United Nations Sustainable Development Goals**, including:

- **SDG 4 — Quality Education**
- **SDG 9 — Innovation**
- **SDG 10 — Reduced Inequality**
- **SDG 16 — Strong Institutions**

---

# 🔥 Core Principle

The mission underlying this research is simple:

> Protect the next generation of thinkers from systems that unintentionally suppress their potential.

CassAI exists to ensure that future AI systems **recognise protectors rather than silence them**.
